---
title: "never know"
date: 2026-03-20
---

Some people are born pretty  
And they will never know  
How all you'll get is pity  
For the ugly scarecrow  

Some people are born loved  
And they will never know  
How it feels to be rejected  
How hard it is to fall  

Some people are born lucky  
And they will never know  
How to lose all money  
In one unexpected blow  

Some people are born happy  
And they will never know  
How it is to bury  
All your traumas deep below  

Some people will never know  
What it's like to live
